# NJSLA-Spring 25 Analysis
Regualr and advanced analysis of Spring 25 NJSLA outcomes comparing testing results based on a variety of variables, time, race, classification, and more.

## Files
- **NJSLA_Spr_25_ELA_Analysis**: Regular analysis of ELA outcomes on the NJSLA assessment.
- **NJSLA_Spr_25_MATH_Analysis**: Regular analysis of MATH outcomes on the NJSLA assessment.
- **NJSLA_Spr_25_ELA_Analysis_SPED**: Analysis of ELA outcomes on the NJSLA assessment of SPED students.
- **NJSLA_Spr_25_MATH_Analysis_SPED**: Analysis of MATH outcomes on the NJSLA assessment of SPED students.
- **NJSLA_Spr_25_ELA_Testing_Time_Multiple_Linear_Regression**: Multiple linear regression exploring test scale score outcomes based on testing time, SPED Classification, and Economic Disadvantage Status in ELA.
- **NJSLA_Spr_25_MATH_Testing_Time_Multiple_Linear_Regression**: Multiple linear regression exploring test scale score outcomes based on testing time, SPED Classification, and Economic Disadvantage Status in MATH.
